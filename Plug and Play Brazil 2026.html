About Plug and Play São Paulo 04 / 05

We connect top startups with corporations rewriting their industries.

We've been investing locally since 2014 and currently hold over 40 active investments across Brazil and the broader Latin American region.

In 2019 we launched our open innovation platform, bringing Silicon Valley expertise and global best practices to corporations and startups alike. Since then, we've connected more than 300 startups with leading companies — matching cutting-edge technology to real-world business challenges.

Our mission is to bring innovation to anyone, anywhere. We continuously build partnerships with academia, civil society, and the public sector — because lasting impact lives beyond traditional corporate–startup relationships.
